### Spotlight 2: AI Play Generator

**Caption:** Type a play description in plain English. PlayForge sends it to Claude AI with full knowledge of every formation, route template, and coordinate rule. Seconds later, you have a complete play diagram with players, routes, and formation -- ready to edit or save. Coaches who hate drawing love this feature.

*Suggested image: Screen recording GIF or screenshot sequence -- typing a description, AI generating, result appearing on canvas.*

### Spotlight 3: Spaced Repetition Learning

**Caption:** PlayForge uses the SM-2 algorithm -- the same spaced repetition engine behind medical school flashcard apps -- to schedule play reviews for each player. Score below 60% on a quiz? That play resets to a 1-day review interval. Score 95%? The interval extends. Four mastery levels (New Play, Learning, Reviewing, Mastered) track real progress, not just time logged.

*Suggested image: Player home screen showing mastery badges on play cards, review queue, and XP progress bar.*

---

### Spotlight 4: Interactive Quizzes

**Caption:** PlayForge quizzes go beyond multiple choice. Five question types test real understanding: tap a zone on the field, identify the route shown, match situations to play calls, recall your specific assignment. Quiz scores feed directly into the spaced repetition system -- struggling plays get scheduled for more review automatically.

*Suggested image: Quiz flow screen showing a tap-field question with the football field and selectable zones.*

**Caption:** The coach dashboard shows install completion (what % of players viewed each play), quiz score averages, a player-by-play mastery heatmap, and a leaderboard with composite scoring (40% mastery, 30% quiz, 20% study time, 10% streak). Inactive player alerts flag anyone who has not logged in for 3+ days. Know exactly who is ready before game day.

## Notes for Content Creators

- **Tone:** Professional but approachable. Speak coach-to-coach, not brand-to-consumer. Avoid hype words ("revolutionary," "disruptive"). Be direct about what the product does.
- **Numbers:** Use specific numbers from the product: 24+ formations, 40+ route templates, 30+ play templates, 7 game formats, 5 quiz types, 10 badges, 7 levels. Specificity builds credibility.
- **Screenshots:** All screenshots should use the dark theme for visual consistency on social media. Show real plays on the canvas, not empty states.
- **Video:** Short screen recordings (15-30 seconds) of the play designer and animation playback perform well on all platforms. GIFs work for Twitter and LinkedIn.
- **Hashtag limit:** Use 3-5 hashtags on Twitter/LinkedIn, up to 15 on Instagram. Always include `#PlayForge` and `#FootballPlaybook`.
