## Feature Showcase

### 1. Play Designer

**Draw plays the way you think about them.**

Open a realistic football field canvas. Choose from 24+ formations for offense and defense. Draw routes by clicking waypoints -- PlayForge auto-detects route types (Go, Slant, Post, Curl, and 36 more). Add pre-snap motion, coverage overlays, and player assignments. Export to PNG or print in playbook sheet and wristband card formats.

*Feature: Drag-and-drop play designer with Konva.js canvas engine*

### 2. AI Play Generator

**Describe it. PlayForge draws it.**

Type "Shotgun trips right, post-corner to the X, slant underneath from the slot" and watch Claude AI generate a complete play diagram with players, routes, and formation -- ready to edit, save, and assign. No drawing required.

*Feature: AI play generation powered by Anthropic Claude*

### 3. Smart Learning

**Spaced repetition, not static PDFs.**

PlayForge uses the SM-2 algorithm to schedule play reviews based on each player's demonstrated mastery. Plays you struggle with come back sooner. Plays you know well fade into longer intervals. The result: long-term retention with minimal daily effort.

Mastery progresses through four levels: New Play, Learning, Reviewing, Mastered. Each level is driven by review performance, not just time spent.

*Feature: SM-2 spaced repetition with mastery tracking*

### 4. Interactive Quizzes

**Test understanding, not just memorization.**

Five question types go beyond multiple choice: tap a zone on the field, identify the route shown, match situations to play calls, and recall your specific assignment. Quiz scores feed directly into the spaced repetition system, so struggling plays get reviewed more often.

*Feature: 5 quiz types integrated with learning system*

### 5. Real-Time Analytics

**Know who is prepared before game day.**

The coach dashboard shows install completion (what percentage of players have viewed each play), average quiz scores, a player-by-play mastery heatmap, and a leaderboard with composite scoring. Get alerts when players are inactive for 3+ days or score below threshold on quizzes.

*Feature: Analytics dashboard with install tracking and mastery heatmaps*

### 6. Every Format

**4v4 flag to 11v11 varsity.**

PlayForge supports 7 game formats with formations that auto-filter to the right player count. Whether you coach 5v5 flag football, 8-man small school football, or full 11v11 varsity, the designer adapts. Youth flag coaches and varsity coordinators use the same powerful tool.

*Feature: 7 game formats with format-specific formations*

## FAQ

### Frequently Asked Questions

**What devices does PlayForge work on?**
PlayForge is a progressive web app (PWA) that works on any modern browser -- phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. You can install it to your home screen for an app-like experience. No app store download required.

**Do my players need to create accounts?**
Yes, but it takes 30 seconds. Share your team's invite code or QR code. Players enter their name, email, and position. They are immediately added to your roster and can see assigned plays.

**What game formats do you support?**
PlayForge supports 7 formats: 4v4, 5v5, 6v6, 7v7, 8v8, 9v9, and 11v11. Formations automatically filter to match the selected format. Whether you coach youth flag or varsity tackle, PlayForge adapts.

**How does the AI play generation work?**
Type a natural language description of the play you want (e.g., "Trips right, slant-flat combo with a deep post"). PlayForge sends this to Claude AI, which generates a complete play diagram with the correct formation, player positions, and routes. You can edit the result in the designer. AI generation requires the Elite plan.

**What is spaced repetition?**
Spaced repetition is a learning technique backed by decades of cognitive science research. Instead of reviewing all plays equally, the SM-2 algorithm schedules each play for review based on how well the player knows it. Plays they struggle with appear more frequently. Plays they have mastered appear less often. The result is better retention with less study time.

**Can I share my playbook with another coach?**
Yes. On the Pro and Elite plans, you can share a playbook with another organization using their team slug or invite code. They can import a full copy of the playbook into their own account. You can revoke access at any time.

**Is there an offline mode?**
PlayForge is PWA-ready with a web app manifest and standalone display mode. Full offline support with background sync is on the Phase 2 roadmap.

**How is PlayForge different from Hudl?**
Hudl is primarily a film review platform. PlayForge is a playbook creation and learning platform. Hudl helps you review what happened on the field. PlayForge helps your team prepare for what will happen next. They are complementary tools -- and PlayForge supports attaching film links to plays for quick reference.

**Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. Your data remains accessible on the free Starter plan if you downgrade.
